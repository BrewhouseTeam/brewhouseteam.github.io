Our Team

Director

Kalvir Sandhu

Kalv has spent the last 15 years shipping products and applications across a variety of industries. Imbuing our company culture with a philosophy of collaboration, transparency, passion, and purpose, Kalv leads the Brewhouse team to develop ideas, software, and rock-solid business strategies for our clients and internal products. He contributes to the Vancouver tech community as the organizer of the #vanruby meetup group.

Software Engineer

Philippe Creux

One of the core contributors of Rails administrative framework ActiveAdmin and an advocate of the Rails community through conferences and a podcast, Philippe has impacted both the development community and the Brewhouse team with his willingness to share and his dedication to writing elegant code. His attention to detail is evident in the intuitive software he creates and the delicious coffee he brews.

Community Manager

Lana Topham

Lana's journey to Brewhouse has taken her from her days writing ads at a small Alaskan radio station, to managing human resources and employee communication at a hosting company, to organizing events, writing blog posts, designing business cards, t-shirts, conference badges, posters, and banners, along with baking the best ruby cookies you've ever tasted for the Brewhouse team and #vanruby meetups. Her humour, creativity, and kindness make Brewhouse a welcoming place for everyone.

Design Director

Pat Dryburgh

Pat found his love for design back in the age of table-based designs and spacer GIFs. Getting his professional start at a small print and web design studio in London, Ontario, Pat's ambition quickly led him to start his own freelancing business working with startups and international brands. His experience allows him to collaborate with designers and developers to craft well-loved products for any platform.

Product Developer

Paulo Ancheta

Paulo Ancheta began his journey at Brewhouse as the very first participant in our apprenticeship program. Paulo is the very definition of a prodigy. An avid musician and former professional chef, Paulo pours his heart and soul into everything he does. In fact, we were so impressed by Paulo's efforts during the 3-month apprenticeship program that we decided to hire him as a full-time employee just two months in.

Product Designer

Lee Nelson

Having followed up his studies in Business Administration and career as a resident DJ with design and development courses at BCIT, Lee Nelson found his passion in solving business problems and building products. His ability to dive deep into a problem and come up with a wonderfully thoughtful design solution has proven invaluable to both our clients and our own products.

Product Developer

Alex Taylor

A film industry veteran, Alex has done everything from guarding parking lots to mastering Hollywood blockbusters. The increasing need for workflow automation sparked his love of software development and since then he has nurtured a growing passion for writing clean and beautiful code. In 2015, he decided to further hone his skills by enrolling in CodeCore's web developer bootcamp, where he excelled at the top of his class. He drinks tea, not coffee, and craft beer, not domestic.

Business Dude

Edward Chan

Having studied Mechatronics Engineering at the University of Waterloo where he spent his days learning how to build robots, Edward made the jump to corporate development at BlackBerry, acquiring startups and striking partnerships. Since then, he has continued his work with startups across sales, growth, and strategy functions. With experience spanning tech, business, and law, Edward heads up the task of growing revenue, developing product partner relationships, and solving business challenges here at Brewhouse.
