--- layout: application title: Meet the Brewhouse Team ---

We believe creating digital products is a craft, like forging a sword from a sheet of steel or brewing that perfect cup of coffee from well-sourced beans. A passion for solving real problems faced by real people is at the heart of why we do what we do, and our transparent, collaborative process brings our team into alignment with your goals and aspirations.

Our team is made up of designers, developers, and product managers who love the process of building products just as much as that perfect cup of coffee.

Director

Kalvir Sandhu

Kalv has spent the last 15 years shipping products and applications across a variety of industries. Imbuing our company culture with a philosophy of collaboration, transparency, passion, and purpose, Kalv leads the Brewhouse team to develop ideas, software, and rock-solid businesses strategies for our clients and internal products. He contributes to the Vancouver tech community as the organizer of #vanruby.

Software Engineer

Godfrey Chan

Since moving to Canada 8 years ago Godfrey has been on a quest to understand technology and the opportunities it provides. Godfrey utilizes his unique blend of computer science, cognitive science, and interaction design knowledge to solve complex problems for client and internal projects, benefiting as well the entire development community through contributions to open source projects — including core Ruby on Rails — and, more recently, his speaking engagements on EmberJS.

Software Engineer

Philippe Creux

One of the core contributors of Rails administrative framework ActiveAdmin and an advocate of the Rails community through conferences and a podcast, Philippe has impacted the development community and the Brewhouse team with his willingness to share and his dedication to writing elegant code. His attention to detail is evident in the intuitive software he creates and the delicious coffee he brews.

Creative Lead

Chuck Bergeron

After studying design, development, photography, and film at SAIT in Calgary, Chuck spent the next decade building creative companies and products both in Canada and abroad. Chuck's deep understanding of the entire stack — from business, design, UI & UX, to front- and back-end development — give him the ability to tame complex digital systems and deliver performant, memorable experiences.

Community Manager

Lana Topham

Lana's journey to Brewhouse has taken her from her days writing ads at a small Alaskan radio station, to managing human resources and employee communication at a hosting company, to organizing events, writing blog posts, designing business cards, t-shirts, conference badges, posters, and banners, and baking the best ruby cookies you've ever tasted for the Brewhouse team and #vanruby meetups. Her humour, and creativity, and kindness make Brewhouse a welcoming place for everyone.

Software Engineer

Gabe Scholz

A former high school physics and mathematics teacher, Gabe found a love of programming by creating irresistible experiences for the web. He designs and develops buttery-smooth interactions with an unwavering focus for writing beautiful, reusable code. Gabe’s desire to teach and raise up the next generation of developers has led him to teach at the local CodeCore bootcamp. Affectionately described by a former student as a JavaScript Wizard, Gabe has spoken about his work at both Vancouver’s AngularJS and VanJS meet ups. He is also an organizer of the #vanruby meet up.

Product Development

Mark Hazlett

Following his time working in the tech support department at Apple with an education in development, Mark continued to build on his experience crafting web applications and mobile experiences as both an employee and an entrepreneur. His understanding and passion for product design and development helps our team and our clients realize the dreams that drive us to build.

Design Director

Pat Dryburgh

Pat found his love for design back in the age of table-based designs and spacer GIFs. Getting his professional start at a small print and web design studio in London, Ontario, Pat's ambition quickly led him to start his own freelancing businesses leading him to work with brands such as Pepsi MAX, Corona, and Adobe. His experience in branding, web design & development, and mobile UX/UI design allow him to collaborate with designers and developers to craft well-loved products for any platform.
