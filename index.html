Community

We <3 Open Source

Our work wouldn’t be possible without of the quality software created by the Open Source software community. We are core committers to projects such as Rails and ActiveAdmin.

#VANRUBY

We are big believers in community. We co-organize the #VANRUBY meetups in Vancouver, bringing disparate programmers together as a whole.

VanAngular JS

We love AngularJS. When it fits the project, we employ Angular's spiffy conventions to polish powerful frontend interfaces. Gabe is a co-organizer of the Vancouver Angular JS meetup, and has written a handful of Angular tools you can use in your projects today.

Ember YVR

We (also) love Ember! Godfrey is a founder and co-organizer of the EmberJS VVR meetups in town, and is always willing to help out with your Ember projects, big or small.
